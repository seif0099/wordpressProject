== Description ==

The Prime Ecommerce Shop WordPress Theme is a powerful, feature-rich template designed specifically for online businesses looking to establish a professional ecommerce presence. Tailored for ecommerce shops, this theme offers an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for anyone to create an online store. Whether you are selling physical products, digital goods, or services, this theme provides the flexibility and functionality you need to succeed. Its visually appealing layout includes customizable product pages, allowing you to showcase items attractively while enhancing the overall shopping experience. With a focus on aesthetics and usability, the Prime Ecommerce Shop theme includes high-quality visuals, smooth navigation, and engaging elements to attract customers. The responsive design ensures your online store looks great on any device, enhancing customer engagement. It supports various ecommerce features, such as shopping carts, multiple payment gateways, and comprehensive product listings, ensuring a seamless checkout process. Additionally, this theme comes with essential tools for ecommerce shop management, including analytics and SEO optimization options, helping you maximise your stores visibility and performance. == Installation ==
        
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the prime-ecommerce-shop theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away
